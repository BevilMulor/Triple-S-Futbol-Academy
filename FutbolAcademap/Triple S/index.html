About Triple S

Triple S Football Academy focuses vastly on soccer.We provide individually designed one-on-one and small-group lessons, team clinics, coach clinics, and even low-key, enjoyable holiday camps for kids of various ages and skill levels.

Our Patron, Michael Olunga, grew up playing Soccer in the Kenyan teams, namely Tusker, Thika, and Gor Mahia, and has since gone on to play professionally abroad in Girona, Kashiwa Reysol, and Al Duhail. Olunga has been passionate about lifting the hands of young players who cannot find the chance to showcase their talents by giving them resources and training from coaches that is vital for their professional development. He has instilled the spirit of giving back to coaches and scouts to nurture young talents.

One of our most significant assets at the Academy is our ability to pinpoint your skill level and provide the guidance, resources, and inspiration you need to improve and ultimately accomplish your objectives!

Michael
Olunga

Michael Olunga's passion for football emerged in his early years in Nairobi. He started gaining attention for his talent while playing for local clubs in Kenya. Starting at youth teams, then graduating to Tusker before heading to Thika United and Gor Mahia drew the eyes of scouts and paved the way for the next chapter in his career.

Olunga in Djurgårdens IF

In 2016, Olunga made a significant move to Europe by joining Swedish club Djurgårdens IF. His time with the club was marked by impressive goal-scoring feats, attracting further interest from clubs across the globe. During his stint in Sweden, Olunga's skill, athleticism, and goal-scoring ability showcased his potential for a successful international career.

Olunga in Girona

Olunga gained international attention during his loan spell at Spanish club Girona F.C. in the 2017-2018 season. His remarkable performances, including a memorable hat-trick against Las Palmas, showcased his goal-scoring prowess on a more prominent stage. On the international stage, Michael Olunga has been a critical figure for the Kenyan national team, Harambee Stars. His contributions, both in terms of goals and leadership, have made him a vital asset to the national squad.

Triple S young talents in Gothic Cup, Sweden

Triple S Football Academy is inspired by the journey of Michael Olunga towards his success in the football profession. He, Olunga, has tried to ensure that young lads acquire exposure to resources and intense games, as this helps boost the players' confidence. In June 2023, the academy players headed to Sweden for Gothic Cup competitions, where some of them were scouted with the European teams and were called for trials. Indeed, Triple S Football Academy is the place to be.

1 On 1 Private Sessions

Everybody aspires to compete at their highest level. You train out at the gym, own the best equipment, and play on the top team, but so does everyone else! How therefore may one obtain a competitive advantage? Working one-on-one with a private coach who specializes in individual growth is the most efficient approach to get better in sports, just like with a tutor in school. We at Triple have years of professional coaching and playing experience, so we know exactly what it takes to improve your game.

Here at TripleS, we have a wealth of expertise and knowledge, which allows us to evaluate a player's current level of performance and create a training plan that will get them there in no time! The main objectives of these 1.5-hour sessions are player development and technical skill, along with soccer-specific agility, balance, coordination, and all other game-related factors. basic sessions also include position-specific drills and workouts in addition to basic game fundamentals.

Play your best with Triple S Football Academy and elevate your game!

Small Group Sessions

Small groups are an excellent opportunity to experience a customized training session with a group of friends or teammates and are structured very similarly to a private one-on-one session. They are naturally a little less intimate than a private session, but the cost is also a little lower and there isn't much of a sacrifice in personalization! With more players on the field, a considerably greater variety of training drills and activities become possible. These rigorous, competitive sessions are a favorite of our TripleS instructors, who want to bring out the best in every player!

Large Group Sessions

These classes are designed especially for players who wish to take advantage of the educational and enjoyable aspects of soccer but do not currently have a team. Or those athletes who play for a team yet require a few additional practice sessions and more touches on the ball each week. We aim to enroll as many participants as we can in these courses and are highly inclusive. More is always better! The main goals of these classes are to impart soccer foundations to the participants and help them advance their technical skills in a pleasant, safe, and enjoyable environment!

Skill Camps

Our week-long school holiday programs, known as Skill Camps, are intended to be enjoyable yet demanding for players of all ages and skill levels. Every morning, our well-trained and seasoned team works closely with the students to improve their basic technique, athleticism, and overall growth as soccer players. We then transition into match and tournament play following a supervised lunch break, giving the players a chance to demonstrate their newly acquired skills in a competitive, match-like setting.
